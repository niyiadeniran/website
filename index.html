Dr Adeniyi Ayobami Adeniran

Public Health Leader • Medical Director • Social Entrepreneur

Epidemiology & Surveillance Expert

A seasoned public health expert with 15+ years experience in disease surveillance & control, epidemiology, health policy and management, and primary health care leadership.

Dr Adeniran has spearheaded responses to HIV/AIDS, Tuberculosis, COVID‑19, Lassa fever, Cholera, Mpox and cVDPV in collaboration with the World Health Organization and other partners. He co‑authored national guidelines, SOPs and strategic plans and has 20+ peer‑reviewed publications.

Qualifications: PhD (Public Health), MPH (Health Policy & Management), MBBS. Fellow of the Royal Society for Public Health; member of Faculty of Public Health (England), EUPHA & ISoP.

Bismo Medical Centre, Ibadan — Medical Director

Established 2013, Bismo is a community‑based primary healthcare facility with two permanent sites and 18 health workers. Over 15,000 patients served, 500+ safe deliveries with zero maternal deaths, and immunization for 400+ children.

Vision: The foremost non‑profit PHC provider in South‑West Nigeria. Mission: Bridging the health‑care gap between rich and poor through our five “E” values: Easy access, Ethical practice, Empathy, Equity, Employment.

Bismo offers subsidized/free TB & HIV services, vocational training for young girls, and jobs for 100+ health workers.

Access Beyond Borders — Chief Executive Officer

Founded in 2023, ABB supports primary healthcare facilities with infrastructure, renewable energy, water supply, commodities and logistics, bridg­ing disparities in underserved communities.

Operating across rural South‑West Nigeria, ABB has delivered renewable energy systems, built maternal centres, empowered adolescent girls, commemorated public‑health days, and spearheaded proposals for global sustainability prizes.
