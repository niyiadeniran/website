About Me

A seasoned public health expert with over 15 years of experience in disease surveillance, epidemiology, health policy, primary health care leadership, and humanitarian response. I've led teams in organizations like WHO to prepare, respond, and control diseases such as HIV/AIDS, TB, COVID-19, and cholera. I hold a PhD in Public Health, an MPH, and an MBBS, and have published over 20 peer-reviewed articles.
